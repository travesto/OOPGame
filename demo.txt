The game uses the following commands:

START

CHECK TIME
LOOK AROUND
NO

A simple demo playthrough would use the following:
START
LOOK AROUND
CHECK TIME
GO NORTH
LOOK AROUND
CHECK TIME
GO EAST
LOOK AROUND
CHECK TIME
NO
GO SOUTH
GO WEST
LOOK AROUND
